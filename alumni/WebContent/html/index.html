ABOUT MIPGS

MIPGS i.e., Matrusri Institute of Post-Graduate Studies, was earlier part of its sister concern MVSR Engineering college. MVSR EC was founded by Matrusri Education Society (MES) in 1981 with three disciplines in engineering. Over the year it has grown from strength to strength and now is a premier technical institute in Andhra Pradesh.

In the due course the Management recognized the growing need of management education with emerging trends in the field of information technology. As a result two P.G. courses viz. MCA and MBA were introduced in MVSR Engineering College during the years 1994 and 1997 respectively.

In the year 1997 MVSR Engineering College was shifted from the present campus to Nadergul village to bigger campus making MIPGS a separate entity in the existing campus under the same management for the two professional P.G. courses viz. MBA and MCA. With excellent track record of placements for its students, MIPGS is a renowned premier institution for professional P.G. course in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
